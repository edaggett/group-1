This app is geared towards those who want to make a difference in a unique, and subtle way. It allows users to interact with each other by assigning dares to one another all across the globe. Taking a humanitarian approach to the original game "Truth or Dare" this app instructs users to perform acts of kindness that are meant to better the world. Users can log their memories to keep track of all of the dares they have completed. The most important features of this app is that users can submit dares which are then randomly generated by the computer to another user. This leaves room for the promotion of creativity and imagination, so that users can add to the enviroment and aid in spreading the kindness.










Do Kind

Our mission is to spread random acts of kindness, one small step at a time. Just a little bit of someone's time is enough to make the world a better place, and our app aims to do exactly that. If you have an idea or want to try out a "dare", sign up for our app and get started!
